Save Barb Game

Click Begin and you will be given a random number.

Below you will see the all four members of the gang from Stranger Things. Each time you click on a member of the gang you will add a specific number of points to your score.

You win the game and save Barb by matching the total score of points to random number given to you at the start of the game. You will lose the game and curse Barb to the creature by going above the random number.

The value of each person is hidden from you until you click on them.

Each time the game starts, the value for each person will change.
